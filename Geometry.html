Learn Section

Here you can learn about specific geometric shapes.

Pyramid

A pyramid is 3d shape that is made up of 4 triangles and a base of a square. It has 5 sides/faces and 5 verticies.

Sphere

A sphere is a 3d shape that is perfectly round. It has no verticies or edges. It is like a circle but in 3d.

Circle

A circle is a 2d shape that is perfectly round. It has no verticies or edges. It is like a sphere but in 2d.

Square

A square is 2d shape it has 4 verticies and all 90 degree right angles.

Triangle

A triangle is a shape with 3 verticies. It has all acute angles. It is a 2d shape.

Cube

A cube is a 3d shape with 8 verticies. It is made up of 6 square shapes. The base is a square. It has 24 90 degree angles.
